The 2011 North Dakota Fighting Sioux football team represented the University of North Dakota in the 2011 NCAA Division I FCS football season. The Fighting Sioux were led by fourth year head coach Chris Mussman and played their home games at the Alerus Center. They are a member of the Great West Conference. This was North Dakota's final year as a member of the Great West as they will become a full member of the Big Sky Conference in 2012.

They finished the season 8–3, 3–1 in Great West play and were conference co-champions, share with Cal Poly.
